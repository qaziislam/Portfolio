About Us

Explore. Create. Capture.

  • Qazi Islam

    My name is Qazi Islam. My obsession started when I was 13 and I hijacked my brothers DSLR. That day I found a new form of communication. Photography to me is the most organic form of communication; it’s the way I speak. The human connection is what I seek to capture while creating reference points in this great story of life for people. Being a creative starts by listening to your inner voice; if you can’t listen then you can’t speak. I hope you hear what I’m talking about and maybe someday we can speak.

  • Tuan Pham

    My name is Tuan. I have picked up a few talents of the years. Learning different forms of movement have allowed me to express myself in a way nothing else could compare. Dancing, b-boying and martial arts tricking all have influenced and shaped me into someone who aspires to better than who I was yesterday. Then I was exposed to the world of film-making and I found one of my callings creating new concepts and perspectives. These two passions have driven me to do more with my life, by inspiring others with what I love to do.

  • Brandon Kuon

    Hello my name is Brandon Kuon, and I’m a photographer for Imagineline. It’s a little funny, since I never imagine myself to take up photography until recent years. Honestly, if you were to ask me four years ago about photography I would have nothing to say about it. But after being exposed to multiple faucets of art, I found myself wanting to express my higher consciousness through an artistic medium. And I have found through merely stumbling into photography which right is a collective hobby for me. I enjoy photography since always engages me to be an advert learner.
